    Hi! I’m Jean-Daniel Borowy.

    Welcome to my home on the web.

    For quite a bit of my time I work on building an automated trading system for an investment management firm. My work is at the intersection of Data Science and Software Engineering, exploring data for strategy development, designing and implementing quantitative tools.

    Prior to joining my current position I worked here developing the pre-trade risk of a low latency trading system and here implementing parts of an asset management application.

    I enjoy solving problems, writing code, investigating data and finding elegant solutions. I’m familiar with financial markets and basic economics and I’m also a individual investor mostly in smallcaps and ETFs following the timeless advices of Benjamin Graham.

    When I’m away from keyboard I spend time as a traveler, runner, climber, snowboarder, amateur photographer. Typography and graphic design are also of great interest to me.
